Childbearing Support

The American Centrist Party supports families at every stage of childbearing and child-rearing, ensuring that parents receive the necessary support to build a strong, stable future.

Incentivizing Parenthood

To encourage and support families, we pledge to:

  • Provide new parents the opportunity to attend a seminar on this plan.
  • Pay the family unit $50,000 tax-free for every child born, with an additional $25,000 tax-free if the parental male is married and cohabitating.
  • Offer 13 weeks of paid parental leave for both parents.
  • Ensure free healthcare from conception through the child’s first two years.
  • Provide a comprehensive birthing class during pregnancy for both parents.
  • If the child is placed for adoption, the birth mother receives $25,000 tax-free.
  • Distribute a neonatal care kit to the mother at birth.
  • Offer free nursing visits up to the child's second year.
  • Provide free infant childcare for the first two years.
  • Ensure access to breastfeeding support if needed.

Parental Responsibilities

In return, parents must meet the following responsibilities:

  • Ensure the child receives all recommended vaccinations.
  • Store any firearms in the home locked, unloaded, and secured with an approved device, out of reach of children.
  • Provide a safe and loving home environment.

Failure to meet these responsibilities terminates eligibility for this program.

Discussion

Our goals with this initiative are:

  • To reduce the number of abortions to near zero in practice.
  • To promote a stable family structure for children.
  • To reverse the national population decline and secure America’s future.
